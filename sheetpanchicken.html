Sheetpan Hot Honey Chicken Bowl

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon pepper, plus more to taste
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika, divided
  • 1 larger (or 2 medium) sweet potatoes, cut into 1/2-inch thick wedges
  • 1/2 medium red onion, cut into 1/2-inch thick wedges
  • 2 tablespoons hot honey, plus more for serving if desired
  • 2 (8.5 ounce) packets microwaveable rice
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 bag crunchy kale salad mix
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (235 degrees C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Toss chicken pieces with 1 tablespoon olive oil and season with 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder and 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika. Spread chicken in an even layer over half the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Toss sweet potatoes, onion, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, remaining garlic powder and remaining paprika until well coated. Add mixture to the other half of the baking sheet. Drizzle honey over chicken and vegetables.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is browned and cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare rice according to package directions.
  6. In a medium bowl whisk together vinegar, remaining olive oil, oregano, and remaining salt and pepper and set aside.
  7. Divide rice, chicken, and sweet potato mixture among 4 bowls.
  8. Carefully pour and whisk reserved drippings from pan into vinegar mixture until well combined. Taste; adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  9. Remove dressing packet from salad mix; save for another use. Add salad mixture to a medium bowl and toss with 2 tablespoons of sauce mixture. Divide salad among the 4 bowls and top each evenly with feta cheese. Drizzle remaining sauce over the bowls.
