This BLT is classic: bacon, lettuce, and tomato â€” nothing fancy, just delicious. (I use fake vegetarian bacon these days, and it's still really good!)

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • Bacon: 4 slices
  • Lettuce: 2 leaves
  • Tomato: 2 slices
  • Bread: 2 slices (toasted)
  • Mayonnaise: 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all Ingredients

  2. Step 2

    Cook bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on a paper towel-lined plate.

  3. Step 3

    Arrange cooked bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices on one slice of bread. Spread mayonnaise on the other slice of bread.

  4. Step 4

    Close to make a sandwich.

  5. Step 5

    Enjoy!
